Why DBAs Script

Every task you do more than twice should be a script. Not because you're lazy — because you're smart.

What DBAs automate:

  • Morning health checks — database status, backups, space, alert log
  • Backup verification — did last night's RMAN backup complete?
  • Space monitoring — tablespace usage, archive log area, disk
  • Log cleanup — rotating and purging old trace files
  • Health reports — daily email with database vital signs
  • User management — batch account creation, expiration checks
  • Scheduled maintenance — stats gathering, index rebuilds

The payoff: I have a script that checks space on every database and emails me at 6am. I used to do that by hand for thirty minutes. Now it does itself, and I read the email with my coffee.
